**The overworld, reforged as endless mountains.** Towering snow-capped peaks, plunging chasm valleys, cliff-face waterfalls — the whole world becomes dramatic high-relief terrain instead of gentle plains. Built on Isekai API.

## What it does

Mountain World turns the overworld into all-mountain terrain — peaks reaching Y 280+, valleys cut down to Y 80, rugged relief everywhere. Every vanilla biome, ore, structure, and mob is remapped into the mountain volume by [Isekai API](https://modrinth.com/mod/isekai-api), so it still feels like Minecraft — just gone vertical.

## Features

- **All-mountain overworld** — towering peaks, deep valleys, cliffs and waterfalls across the whole world.
- **Altitude-aware structures** — lowland villages settle in the valleys (Y 80-140); snowy villages perch high on the slopes (Y 200-280); strongholds stay deep underground.
- **Vanilla content preserved** — ores, structures, and mobs are remapped into the mountain terrain (no empty worlds).
- **Ocean cleanup** — ocean monuments, shipwrecks, and ruins are excluded so the peaks stay clean.

## Requires

**Isekai API** (required) — Mountain World is a datapack-style worldshape built entirely on Isekai's worldgen primitives.

## Compatibility

Coexists with TerraBlender, YUNG's structure mods, map mods (Journeymap etc.), and dimension mods like Nullscape. Mutually exclusive with other overworld-overhaul mods (Terralith, William Wythers') — only one mod can own the overworld; no crash, last-loaded wins.
